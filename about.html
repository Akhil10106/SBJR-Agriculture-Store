About Us

Suraj Bhan Jagdish Rai is an Agriculture Store, Co-Founded by Gulshan Goel. SBJR Agriculture Store was established in 2006 in Chhachhrauli, Yamunanagar district, Haryana, India. Previously, the SBJR store was located in front of Shero Wala Mandir, Chhachhrauli. However, on February 5, 2020, SBJR changed its store location and is now situated in New Market, Ponta Sahib road, Chhachhrauli, Yamunanagar district, Haryana, India.

SBJR Agriculture Store deals with all kinds of Seeds, Pesticides, and Fertilizers, providing high-quality products along with quantity. Farmers trust us and believe in our products. SBJR collaborates with famous and best-quality product companies such as Mankind, Indofil, Indogulf, etc. We have agencies of many companies, including the ones mentioned above.

The main vision of SBJR is to provide the best and high-quality products to farmers, a tradition maintained for over 90 years. While SBJR was a 90-year-old farm in the Aadat industry (Anaaj Mandi), from 2006, it focused solely on Seeds, Pesticides, and Fertilizers. Due to our 90 years of history, all farmers within a range of 15km know us and trust our products. We possess great experience in the Agriculture Industry.

SBJR stands for Suraj Bhan Jagdish Rai.
